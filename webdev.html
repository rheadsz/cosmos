Anywhere Door

This item helps to travel from one destination to another. One needs to say where they wish to go, thus opening the door and go through it to reach their destination.

Computer Pencil

This is a 'smart' pencil. When one writes something with it, it will write the correct answer no matter what (though blank papers are shown to have no effects).

Take-copter

It looks like a miniature helicopter blade spinning when its in action.

It is used to enable flight by attaching it to the user's head. To fly you need to press the white button on top of the take-copter. How to use: Just imagine the direction and speed and your thought will be transmitted to the machine.

Invisibility Cloak

This magical item helps in making you invisible as soon as you wrap it around yourself. This would be very useful in times of war as your attacker is approaching you.

Magnify Ray

This gadget can increase the size of any item or person immensley. Once enough time has passed (24hrs) the user will return to their original height per the use of the gadget.

Time Machine

By using this, one can travel through both time and space. One may decide the portal of access of one's time machine, homologous to a pier or parking place in common transport.

Translator Jelly

This tool is very helpful for people who travel to various places and aren't well versed with the language of different localities. By using this translator jelly the language the person speaks in will be translated accordingly depending on which place the person is in.

Scheduler Clock

This is a very helpful gadget for students. It is used to plan events of any kind. Once a schedule is inserted in the gadget, it forces the user to do the event by hook or by crook
